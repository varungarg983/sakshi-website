# LinkedIn Post Templates

Use these as repeatable frameworks.

## Template 1: Practical tip

Hook:
If you are thinking about selling your business, start here.

Body:
One of the first things buyers look for is clarity.

That means:
- clear financials
- clear operations
- clear reasons for recent performance

If those areas are messy, buyer confidence usually drops quickly.

Small improvements before going to market can make a big difference.

CTA:
If you are thinking about selling in the next 12 to 24 months, send me a message.

## Template 2: Common mistake

Hook:
One mistake many business owners make before selling:

Body:
They wait until they are ready to exit before they start preparing.

In reality, the strongest sale processes usually start earlier.

That gives you time to:
- tidy the numbers
- reduce owner dependence
- improve the way the business is presented

CTA:
If you want a clearer sense of readiness, try the sale readiness check.

## Template 3: Buyer education

Hook:
Buying a business is not just about liking the idea of it.

Body:
A buyer should understand:
- where the revenue comes from
- how dependent the business is on the owner
- whether the margins are sustainable
- what risks sit behind the headline numbers

A good opportunity should still stand up after the detail is reviewed.

CTA:
If you are looking at business opportunities in New Zealand, feel free to get in touch.

## Template 4: Personal trust post

Hook:
One thing I have learnt in business sales:

Body:
People do not just want information.

Buying or selling a business can feel overwhelming if the process is unclear.

That is why I try to keep communication practical, direct, and easy to understand.

Hook:
Not every business is ready to go to market straight away.

That is not bad news.
