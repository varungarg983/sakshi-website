# LinkedIn Content Pillars

## Main goal

Generate seller leads, buyer conversations, and trust.

## Audience

- New Zealand business owners thinking about selling
- Buyers exploring businesses for sale
- People who want practical, plain-English guidance

## Recommended pillars

### 1. Selling a business

Purpose:
Attract business owners who may sell in the next 6 to 24 months.

Topics:

- signs a business is not ready for sale
- what buyers ask first
- common seller mistakes
- timing a sale
- what clean numbers look like

### 2. Buying a business

Purpose:
Attract buyer enquiries and build credibility with buyers.

Topics:

- what buyers should check before making an offer
- red flags in small business purchases
- how to assess owner dependence
- questions to ask about revenue quality

### 3. Process and education

Purpose:
Reduce confusion and show expertise.

Topics:

- what a business broker actually does
- what happens in a sale process
- what confidentiality means
- how a buyer is qualified

### 4. Personal trust and reputation

Purpose:
Make Sakshi more known and more credible.

Topics:

- lessons from working with buyers and sellers
- communication style and approach
- why clear advice matters
- multilingual communication and working with diverse clients

## Content mix

- 40% seller content
- 25% buyer content
- 20% educational process content
- 15% personal trust content

## CTA style

Keep CTAs soft and simple:

- Send me a message if you are thinking about selling
- If you want a private conversation, get in touch
- If you are exploring a purchase, happy to chat
- Try the sale readiness check
