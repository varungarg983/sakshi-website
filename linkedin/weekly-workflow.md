# Weekly LinkedIn Workflow

## Goal

Publish consistently without creating content from scratch every day.

## Weekly rhythm

### Monday

- choose 3 topics from `30-post-ideas.csv`
- draft 3 posts using `post-templates.md`

### Tuesday

- edit the posts to sound natural and personal
- add one soft CTA to each post

### Wednesday

- publish seller-focused post
- spend 15 minutes replying to comments

### Thursday

- publish buyer or educational post
- engage with 5 to 10 relevant LinkedIn posts

### Friday

- publish personal trust-building post
- review which post got the most profile visits or conversations

## Monthly review

At the end of each month, note:

- which hooks got comments
- which posts led to profile visits
- which posts led to DMs or enquiries
- whether seller or buyer content performed better

## Best first automation setup

### Level 1

- batch-write posts weekly
- schedule posts manually
- track results in a spreadsheet

### Level 2

- use a LinkedIn-compatible scheduler
- maintain a 2-week content queue
- reuse strong posts in new formats

### Level 3

- connect website leads and LinkedIn content themes
- use the readiness check as a recurring CTA
- build a content library from FAQs and real buyer or seller questions
