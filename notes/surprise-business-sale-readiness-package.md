# Surprise Asset: Business Sale Readiness Package

## Why this idea

Instead of another generic "free appraisal" angle, this offer gives sellers a lower-pressure first step:

**The 15-Minute Business Sale Readiness Check**

It works because it:
- feels practical, not salesy
- attracts owners earlier, before they are fully committed to selling
- builds trust by showing process knowledge
- creates a natural path into a valuation, strategy call, or listing conversation

This is especially useful for small and medium business owners in New Zealand who know they may want to sell, but are unsure whether they are actually ready.

---

## Offer Summary

**Lead magnet name**
The 15-Minute Business Sale Readiness Check

**Format**
Short checklist PDF or simple web form assessment

**Primary audience**
Business owners in New Zealand thinking about selling in the next 6 to 24 months

**Primary CTA**
Get the Checklist

**Recommended form fields**
- First name
- Email
- Are you thinking of selling within 24 months? `Yes / Maybe / Just exploring`
