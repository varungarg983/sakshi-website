## Lead Generation Strategy

We first discussed a broader lead generation approach using `marketing-ideas`, including:
- seller landing pages
- Google Search
- LinkedIn content
- lead magnets
- referral partners

You then clarified that all sold businesses so far have come through social media reels on TikTok, Facebook, and Instagram.

That changed the strategy:
- short-form video is the main lead engine
- social content should focus more on seller education and seller intent
- every reel should drive to a clear CTA such as `Message me`
- follow-up should happen through DMs and consultation booking
