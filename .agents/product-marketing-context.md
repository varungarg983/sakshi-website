# Product Marketing Context

## Business
Sakshi Aggarwal is a business broker in New Zealand who helps people buy and sell businesses.

## Target Customers
- Business owners who want to sell their business
- Buyers looking to purchase a business in New Zealand
- People exploring cafes, retail, hospitality, service businesses, and other small to medium businesses

## Problems We Solve
- Owners do not know how to sell their business properly
- Sellers struggle to find serious buyers
- Buyers do not know how to assess opportunities
- Business sales can feel confusing, slow, and risky
- Many people need help understanding process, pricing, and presentation

## Positioning
Professional, approachable, practical business brokerage support for buyers and sellers in New Zealand.

## Goals
- Generate seller leads
- Generate buyer enquiries
- Build trust through informative content
- Create landing pages, listing copy, and social content that convert

## Tone
Clear, trustworthy, practical, confident, and easy to understand.

## Geography
New Zealand

## Audience Notes
Write for everyday business owners and buyers, not corporate finance experts.
